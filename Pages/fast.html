About the Movie

Fast X (also known as Fast & Furious 10) is a 2023 American action film directed by Louis Leterrier from a screenplay written by Dan Mazeau and Justin Lin, both of whom also co-wrote the story with Zach Dean. It is the sequel to F9 (2021), the main tenth and penultimate installment, and the eleventh installment overall in the Fast & Furious franchise.
